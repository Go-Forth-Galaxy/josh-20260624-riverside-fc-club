About the Club

More than a team — a city's heartbeat.

Founded in 2009 by a handful of friends on the banks of the river, Riverside FC has grown from a Sunday-league side into one of the region's most respected community clubs. We've never lost sight of where we came from: the same streets, the same supporters, the same belief.

From our youth academy to the first team, every player wears the navy and electric green with pride. We compete hard on the pitch and invest deeply off it — running free coaching clinics, community days, and pathways for the next generation of Riverside talent.

Home matches are played at Riverside Park Stadium, where 8,500 members make it one of the loudest grounds in the league.
